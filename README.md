# People Risk Analytics — Employee Attrition Analysis

## Business Question
Which employees are most at risk of leaving — and what is it costing the company?

## Key Findings
- 16% attrition rate — 1 in 6 employees is leaving
- 3 of the top 5 attrition drivers are compensation-related
- Highest-risk profile: young, lower-paid Sales employees 
  in their first 5 years, working overtime
- Estimated annual cost of attrition: $31.2M (SHRM benchmark)

## Deliverables
- Full EDA + Random Forest model (62% recall on leavers)
- Spurious variable identification and removal
- 2-page People Risk Report (see /report)

## Tools
Python, pandas, scikit-learn, matplotlib, seaborn

## Dataset
IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition — Kaggle
